# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for Olist E-commerce Analysis

## 1. Revenue & Orders KPIs
- **Total Revenue (GMV)**  
  `SUM(price + freight_value)`
- **Monthly Revenue**
- **Monthly Orders**
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**  
  `Revenue / Number of Orders`
- **Revenue per Customer**

## 2. Customer KPIs
- **New Customers per Month**
- **Repeat Customer Rate**
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) â€” simplified**
- **Customer Retention Rate**
- **Churn Rate**

## 3. Delivery & Logistics KPIs
- **Average Delivery Time**
- **Delivery Delay Rate**  
  `% of orders delivered after estimated delivery date`
- **Average Estimated Delivery vs Actual Delivery Gap**

## 4. Product KPIs
- **Top 10 Categories by Revenue**
- **Categories with Highest Cancel/Refund**
- **Categories with Lowest Review Scores**
- **Revenue per Product Category**

## 5. Payment KPIs
- **Payment Installments Distribution**
- **Payment Type Share (Credit, Debit, Voucher, etc.)**

## 6. Satisfaction KPIs
- **Average Review Score**
- **% of 1- or 2-star reviews**
- **Delivery delay impact on review score**

---

These KPIs will guide your SQL work and all dashboards later.
