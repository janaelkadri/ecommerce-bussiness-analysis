## 1. Product Performance
- Which product categories and SKUs drive the highest revenue and order volume?
- Which products underperform based on sales, reviews, or return/cancellation rates?
- How does product pricing compare across similar categories or substitutes?
- What products generate high demand but low profitability?

## 2. Customer Behavior & Retention
- What is the overall customer retention rate and churn rate?
- How do customer segments differ in spending, frequency, and lifetime value?
- Which customer cohorts (based on first purchase month) retain the longest?
- Which product categories are favored by high-value vs low-value customers?

## 3. Seller (Merchant) Performance
- Which sellers generate the most revenue, highest ratings, and best delivery performance?
- How does delivery speed vary by seller, and how does it impact review scores?
- Which sellers consistently have high cancellation or complaint rates?

## 4. Delivery & Logistics
- What is the average delivery time across regions?
- Which states/cities experience the longest delivery delays?
- How strongly does delivery delay influence customer satisfaction?

## 5. Payments & Order Behavior
- Which payment methods and installment patterns are most common?
- Do installment payments correlate with higher order values?
- Are certain payment types associated with higher cancellation or late payment issues?

## 6. Strategic Insights & Opportunities
- What are the main drivers of revenue growth or decline?
- Which improvements (product, delivery, seller, customer experience) offer the highest ROI?
- What actions can improve retention and reduce churn?
